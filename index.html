About Us

Our aim is to improve the lives of our customers by providing them with plants to liven up their homes, as well as giving tips on how to care for them.

What makes us unique

We make sure that our plants are delivered in the best condition possible. When buying plants, they spend around 4 weeks being shipped to a warehouse in a truck, being exposed to severe temperatures and not getting the needed water and light. With Plantopia, our plants are cared for by botanical experts and are shipped directly to your doorstep. This means that your plants will be delivered to you in the best condition possible.
















Guidance

Plantopia is here to help you with any and all of your plantcare questions. We provide care instructions for every plant you purchase, to ensure that you will be able to care for them on your own. For more support, feel free to email us any questions you may have!
